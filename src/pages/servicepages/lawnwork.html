Comprehensive Lawn Care & Smart Irrigation Systems

Healthy Lawns Begin with Professional Maintenance and Efficient Watering

Lawn Mowing, Fertilizing, Aeration & More

A lush, green lawn doesn't happen by accident — it takes a consistent, well-rounded care plan tailored to your property's specific needs. At New Leaf Landscaping, we offer professional lawn care services that go far beyond simple mowing. From regular trimming and edging to aeration and fertilization, we address every factor that affects the health and appearance of your lawn. We analyze your soil, grass type, sun exposure, and foot traffic to create a lawn care schedule that maximizes growth and minimizes weed competition.

Our goal is to enhance the curb appeal of your property and create a thick, vibrant lawn you can enjoy all season long. Whether you're maintaining an existing yard or trying to bring neglected grass back to life, our team has the experience and equipment to get the job done right — reliably, efficiently, and affordably.

What’s Included in Our Lawn Maintenance Plans

  • Weekly or bi-weekly lawn mowing with professional-grade equipment
  • Edging and trimming for clean lines along driveways, sidewalks, and beds
  • Fertilization and weed control tailored to your soil’s pH and lawn type
  • Overseeding to promote fuller, denser grass coverage
  • Seasonal aeration to relieve soil compaction and encourage healthy root systems

Complete Irrigation System Services

  1. Complete sprinkler system installation for lawns, gardens, and planting beds
  2. Smart irrigation controllers that adjust based on weather and soil moisture
  3. Drip irrigation options for efficient garden watering
  4. Spring start-up and fall winterization services to protect your system
  5. Troubleshooting, leak repair, and system optimization for peak performance
