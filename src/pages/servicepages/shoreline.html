Shoreline Stabilization in Michigan Well-Being

Protect Your Property & Preserve Nature with Expert Erosion Control Solutions

At New Leaf Landscaping, we specialize in shoreline stabilization services designed to combat erosion, protect waterfront properties, and restore the natural beauty of your lakefront. Whether you’re dealing with crumbling banks, rapid erosion, or storm damage, our team uses proven techniques like riprap rock placement, native vegetation, and engineered barriers to ensure long-lasting results.

Why Shoreline Stabilization Matters

  1. Property loss and damage
  2. Harm to aquatic ecosystems
  3. Unsafe conditions for people and pets

Custom Solutions for Every Waterfront

We work with homeowners, HOAs, and municipalities to develop tailored plans that suit your specific shoreline conditions. From gentle slopes to steep banks, we’ve stabilized hundreds of Michigan shorelines using:

  • Riprap stone barriers
  • Bioengineering with native plants
  • Erosion control fabrics & grading
