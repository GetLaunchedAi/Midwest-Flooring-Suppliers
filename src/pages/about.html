About Us

About AIMAI

AIMAI Auto Electronic Solutions was founded by Isaiah D. Alexander, a passionate and self-taught technician from Inkster, Michigan. With early hands-on experience and formal education from ITT Technical Institute, Isaiah transformed his lifelong passion for electronics into a successful mobile installation business. After facing and overcoming adversity, he officially launched AIMAI in 2022 and has since serviced thousands of vehicles and heavy equipment—from daily drivers and commercial fleets to generators and agricultural machinery.

Supported by MECP certifications and extensive field experience, Isaiah now leads installations for national brands and mentors the next wave of technicians. His mission: engineer trust, restore confidence, and deliver intelligent solutions for anything powered by a battery and motor.

I’m Isaiah D. Alexander, founder of AIMAI Auto Electronic Solutions. I’ve spent decades turning a passion for electronics into a full-scale mobile installation service. From humble beginnings and hands-on experience as a kid to earning technical certifications and leading national installs, I’ve built this business to deliver precision, reliability, and innovation to every job.
