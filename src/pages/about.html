About AIMAI

AIMAI Auto Electronic Solutions was founded by Isaiah D. Alexander, a passionate and self-taught technician from Inkster, Michigan. With hands-on experience dating back to childhood and formal training from ITT Technical Institute, Isaiah turned a lifelong fascination with electronics into a thriving mobile installation business. After overcoming personal setbacks, he launched AIMAI in 2022 and has since serviced thousands of vehicles and heavy equipment—from sedans and semi-trucks to generators and farm machinery.

Backed by MECP certifications and years of real-world problem solving, Isaiah now leads installations for national brands and trains the next generation of technicians. His mission: engineer trust, restore confidence, and bring intelligent solutions to anything with a battery and motor.

I’m Isaiah D. Alexander, founder of AIMAI Auto Electronic Solutions. I’ve spent decades turning a passion for electronics into a full-scale mobile installation service. From humble beginnings and hands-on experience as a kid to earning technical certifications and leading national installs, I’ve built this business to deliver precision, reliability, and innovation to every job.

Isaiah D. Alexander Founder & Lead Technician
